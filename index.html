About

I am currently an assistant professor in Operations, Technology, and Information Management at the Johnson Graduate School of Management. I received my PhD from the Operations, Information, and Decisions (OID) department at the University of Pennsylvania's Wharton School, where I was advised by professor Hamsa Bastani . My research is centered around studying adaptive learning algorithms, primarily for healthcare operations. My work thus far has focused on applications to the design of clinical trials and public health intervention schemes, as well as , though it is more widely applicable to other areas as well.
